Build Your Audience in Your Sleep

You don't have to be onstage to get business, but your website does. It must reflect who you are, look great on any device, and stay current. Learn to Tap Dance

I have over fifteen years of experience teaching tap dance technique and have taught students of all ages and levels. Not only will I develop your technique and movement quality, but also your relationship to music. We will also explore your individual voice through improvisation and discuss the history of tap dance, an art form that owes itself to the contributions of African American dancers and musicians.
